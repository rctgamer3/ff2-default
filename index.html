Firefox 2, the theme, reloaded by rctgamer3 and fax8

Full power of the new Gecko 2 engine with a good old Firefox 2 face.

Firefox 2 theme for Firefox 4 (and up) with some customizations, for (even) better results use the oldbar and Status-4-Evar extensions.
